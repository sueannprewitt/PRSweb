WELCOME TO PURCHASE REQUEST SYSTEM

This website is designed for in-house staff/users of a company to use to order needed supplies. The types of users are admins, users, and reviewers. Certain staff can be an admin and/or a reviewer which gives them special privileges within the website.

The website lists the users, vendors, products, and purchase requests. Requests are made creating a new purchase request. New requests are set for review. Once reviewed, they are approved or rejected. Purchase requests that are $50.00 or less are automatically approved by the system.
